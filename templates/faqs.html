Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Career Assistant?

AI Career Assistant is a smart platform designed to help job seekers improve their resumes, assess fit for specific roles, and practice mock interviews using AI-driven insights.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use encrypted storage and industry-standard security protocols. Your data is never sold or shared without consent.

Can I update or delete my uploaded data?

Yes, your dashboard allows you to manage, edit, or delete any uploaded documents at any time.

How accurate are the mock interviews?

Our AI simulates real interview patterns using role-specific prompts and feedback. While not a replacement for human interviews, it offers a close approximation and helps you prepare effectively.

Do I need to pay to use the platform?

The core features are free. Premium features, like advanced analytics or unlimited mock interviews, may require a subscription in the future.
