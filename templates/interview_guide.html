Interview Preparation Guide

  1. Research thoroughly: Understand the company, role responsibilities, and recent developments.
  2. Master STAR method: Practice situational questions using Situation, Task, Action, and Result framework.
  3. Review your resume: Be ready to explain each line, especially achievements and transitions.
  4. Prepare questions: Ask insightful questions about the team, growth opportunities, and culture.
  5. Mock interviews: Simulate the environment with peers or platforms like AI Career Assistant.
  6. Mind your etiquette: Dress appropriately, be punctual, and maintain polite body language.
