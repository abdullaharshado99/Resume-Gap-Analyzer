Your data is used solely for analysis and providing feedback. We do not share or sell your information to third parties without your consent.

All data is encrypted during transmission and stored using secure cloud practices. We employ access control and monitoring to protect against unauthorized access.

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may delete your information at any time from your dashboard.

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data. Contact support if you need assistance.

6. Changes to Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
